The Invisible Architecture • 08-02-2025 • Author: Yazedy Yahya

How Digital Spaces Shape Human Behavior. In the physical world, we instinctively understand how architecture influences our actions. A cathedral's soaring ceilings inspire reverence; a cozy café encourages lingering conversations. But what about the digital spaces we inhabit for hours each day? These environments lack physical dimensions yet profoundly shape our behaviors, decisions, and experiences. Every website, app, and digital platform we use has been deliberately designed. Behind each interface stands a team of invisible architects who have made countless decisions about how we will navigate, interact, and engage.

Beyond Usability • 25-01-2025 • Author: Yazedy Yahya

The Ethics of Persuasive Design. In today's digital landscape, creating products that are merely usable is no longer enough. Designers are increasingly expected to craft experiences that actively influence user behavior—encouraging more engagement, driving conversions, or promoting certain actions. This shift from passive usability to active persuasion raises important questions about the ethical boundaries of design. Persuasive design leverages psychology and behavioral economics to guide users toward specific outcomes. It's the science behind why we keep scrolling through social media feeds, why we complete loyalty programs, and why we respond to certain calls-to-action over others.
